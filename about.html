About Us



Honeywest

Our company — Honeywest Global Ventures Limited is a private business , founded to meet the demands of her customers locally and internationally, as one of the trusted wholesaler, exporter, importer, distributor and agent that provides a centralized service for the purchase of wide variety of agricultural products and to provide the best quality goods and customer service in the industry.

Our company offers the Export & Import of Dry Fruits : Raw Cashew Nuts, Walnuts. Vegetables : Ginger, Sesame Seeds, Cocoa, Soybeans etc.
We also are involved in the Export of locally Nigerian made snacks and food such as Kuli Kuli(Sugared and salted Groundnut Cake) , Dry Ponmo (Cow Skin) , Garri (Cassava Flakes), Cray Fish and even Cat Fish.

Honeywest Global Ventures Limited has a sister company named Peniel Agro Industry Farms , we are involved in the production of livestock feeds and their products See more...




Vision

To be the best Nigerian nut trading company to supply premium quality nuts worldwide through a dedicated and innovating service and to also become the foremost and the highest benchmark setter in the cashew nuts supplying industry catering to both, the domestic and international markets.

Mission

To achieve our vision, we intend to deal in the best business practices. Therefore, we will build a solid business structure that will see us employing only competent and dedicated employees that understand our corporate goals and objectives.

OUR CORE VALUES

Inspiring trust and confidence through maintaining ethical and professional standards, saying what we mean, doing what we say, and taking responsibility for our actions.
Striving to deliver on our commitments with our client’s needs in mind, on time, and to the highest standard.
Fostering a collaborative way of working through information sharing, actively listening, and giving constructive challenge.
We also conduct ourselves with a policy of utmost good faith and as a result, we are determined to provide our clients with the best solution for their business.
We strive to be driven, attentive, and innovative, and always deliver excellence.



Peniel Agro Industry Farms

Peniel Agro Industry Farms is a private business , founded to meet the demands of her customers locally , we are involved in the rearing and production of live stock such as Chickens (Layers , Broilers) , Pigs , Cows , Sheep , catfishes and mammal Goats , as one of the trusted livestock production company that provides a centralized service for the purchase of wide variety of agricultural and livestock products and to provide the best quality goods and customer service in the industry. we are commited to a sustainable, environmental friendly agriculture.
